- Client functionality:
	There are two types of animals action in the store:
	1. Buying animals. (Store)
	2. Rent, Borrowing, Sitting (Animal sitter)
--- 
- Selling: 
	1. List of available animals.
	2. Characteristics, animal description (animal page).
	3. Recommendation additional goods. 
	4. Online / Local payment
- Boarding: 
	1. Registration  / Authorization required (A lot of contact info).
	2. Fill out the boarding form
		1. Include - Register pet / Take from the list.
		2. Include - Selection of feeding type.
		3. Include - Selection of period time.
		4. Include - Payment type.
	3. List of boarded pets.

- Admin functionality:
	1. List of animals to sell (CRUD).
	2. List of products (CRUD).
	3. Order products 
	4. Check forms
	5. Ban client.
	6. Admin should authorizate into system.
