## Level Note Standard

Every level note must contain:
1. **Overview** — one-paragraph summary of the level's design intent
2. **Objectives** — player goals and win/fail conditions
3. **Flow** — embedded Excalidraw canvas (auto-generated from template)
4. **Mechanic Spotlight** — which mechanics are introduced or tested in this level
5. **Technical Notes** — performance budget, streaming zones, LOD targets
6. **Status** — `concept | blockout | greybox | art-pass | shipped`
